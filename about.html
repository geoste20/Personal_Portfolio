ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Giorgos Stefanis, and I am a Computer Science graduate from Queens College, City University of New York.

I was born and raised in Cyprus and moved to the United States over five years ago to chase my dream of building a future in technology.

Moving to a new country taught me independence, resilience, and the importance of working hard for the life I want to create. These experiences shaped me both personally and professionally.

I enjoy building software, creating websites, working with databases, and learning new technologies. I am especially interested in software engineering, web development, cloud computing, and databases.

Before focusing fully on technology, I used to be a Track & Field athlete. Athletics taught me discipline, consistency, and how to stay focused under pressure.

Outside of coding, I enjoy running, staying active, traveling, and spending time with friends. Having lived in both Cyprus and New York, I enjoy meeting people from different backgrounds and learning about their experiences and cultures.

My goal is to begin my career as a Software Engineer and keep growing as a developer while contributing to meaningful and innovative projects.
